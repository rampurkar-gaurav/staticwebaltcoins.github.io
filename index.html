Ethereum

One of the Top 3 cryptocurrencies

was proposed in 2013 by programmer Vitalik Buterin.

Ethereum Features
  • Ether: This is Ethereum’s cryptocurrency.
  • Smart contracts: Ethereum allows the development and deployment of these.
  • Ethereum Virtual Machine: Ethereum provides the underlying technology—the architecture and the software—that understands smart contracts and allows you to interact with it.
  • Decentralized applications (Dapps): Ethereum allows you to create consolidated applications, called decentralized applications. A decentralized application is called a Dapp (also spelled DAPP, App, or DApp) for short.
  • Decentralized autonomous organizations (DAOs): Ethereum allows you to create these for democratic decision-making.
  • Ethereum Virtual Machine.
  • If you love smart contracts, you're gonna adore Ethereum.
  • Ethereum is one of the hottest programming development tools out there.

    • "It's a centralized decentralized currency."

Litecoin

An early bitcoin

was proposed by Charlie Lee, a Google employee.

Litecoin facts
  • Transactions on the Litecoin network happen faster.
  • It is a fully decentralized, open-source, global payment network.
  • Lee developed Litecoin with the aim to improve on Bitcoin's shortcomings.
  • Only approximately 84 million litecoins will ever be created, quadruple the total bitcoin supply.
  • .Litecoin also creates new blocks every 2.5 minutes

    • "Litecoin is the Silver to Bitcoin’s Gold"

Dogecoin

Booming cryptocurrency

was proposed by engineers Billy Markus and Jackson Palmer.

Dogecoin facts
  • Dogecoin initially started as a joke based on a popular meme featuring a Shiba Inu (a Japanese breed of dog).
  • It is based on Litecoin and has the same technology behind its proof-of-work.
  • Dogecoin is an open-source cryptocurrency.
  • Dogecoin is unlimited.
  • The Dogecoin mining process is one of the fastest.

Monero

a privacy-focused cryptocurrency

Founded by a group of users led by Johnny Mnemonic

Monero facts
  • Monero is a privacy based crypto-"XMR".
  • The stealth address feature is a huge upgrade over bitcoin's privacy
  • A ring signature is a way to make sure a transaction can’t be tied back to a specific individual
  • You can share your public-view key so other people can see your transactions
  • Monero will have a set coin supply and programmed inflation rate
  • Built-in Scalability
  • Monero introduced the RandomX proof of work algorithm in November 2019 to further increase resistance to ASIC mining

    • "most secure cryptocurrency"
